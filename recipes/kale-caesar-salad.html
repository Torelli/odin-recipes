Kale Caesar Salad

Gotta love those croutons!

A Caesar salad (also spelled Cesar and Cesare) is a green salad of romaine lettuce and croutons dressed with lemon juice (or lime juice), olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. In its original form, this salad was prepared and served tableside

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 oil-packed anchovy fillets
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese (Optional)
  • 1 bunch kale, ribs removed and leaves torn into pieces
  • 1 cup Caesar salad croutons
  • 2 tablespoons shaved Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese (Optional)

Steps

  1. Place mayonnaise, lemon juice, garlic, anchovies, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper in the bowl of a food processor.
  2. Transfer to a small bowl and stir in grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese. Set aside.
  3. Place kale in a large salad bowl. Using your hands, massage the kale to soften slightly.
  4. Add a few dollops of the dressing on top of the kale, and stir until all pieces are lightly coated with the dressing.
  5. Sprinkle with croutons and top with shaved Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.
